Sri Lanka


Sri Lanka is a popular tourist destination in South Asia, renowned for its diverse landscape including stunning beaches, ancient ruins like Sigiriya, rich Buddhist culture with sites like the Temple of the Tooth in Kandy, abundant wildlife in national parks like Yala, and lush tea plantations, making it a great choice for those seeking a mix of history, nature, and relaxation.
